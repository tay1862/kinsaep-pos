FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about BNOS.

What is BNOS?

BNOS (Bitcoin Nostr Operating System) is a decentralized, lightning-powered Point of Sale (POS) and business operating system. It allows businesses to accept Bitcoin payments and manage their operations without intermediaries.

Is BNOS free to use?

Yes, BNOS is open-source software. You can use the basic features for free. We do not charge transaction fees on your payments.

Do I need a bank account?

No, you do not need a traditional bank account. BNOS operates on the Bitcoin Lightning Network, allowing you to have full control over your funds.

How does offline mode work?

BNOS uses a 'Local-First' architecture. Data is stored on your device first. You can process sales offline, and data will sync with Nostr relays when you reconnect.
